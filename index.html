About

I am a research scientist specializing in artificial intelligence and digital finance. My research focuses on the intersection of deep learning and social network analysis, with applications in financial technology and technology valuation.

I hold a Ph.D. in Management of Technology and an MBA in Digital Finance. My work combines theoretical foundations with practical applications, particularly in developing AI-powered solutions for financial analysis and technology assessment.

Recent Projects

ABP Project: A comprehensive research initiative combining deep learning techniques with social network analysis to understand complex financial ecosystems. The project utilizes PyTorch, NetworkX, and AWS Bedrock for scalable AI solutions.

Technology Valuation Framework: Development of machine learning models for assessing technology value in digital finance applications.
